About

I'm Daniel Ndanema, a student at the Limkokwing University of Creative Technology, Cyberjaya Campus.

I am pursuing a course in BSc. (Hons) Software Engineering with Multimedia. My expected graduation is in 2023.

I love reading; I will be writing down my thoughts and also keep meaningful notes and resources I find on this site. I will be writing about my course content and other concepts which I might find stimulating.

I am learning website creation and other design aspects, and I'm excited to put it to practice and see what I can achieve.
